ECB publishes indicative operational calendars for 2024 | 2023-09-15 | European Central Bank | Press Release

The European Central Bank (ECB) today published the indicative calendars for the Eurosystem’s regular tender operations and reserve maintenance periods in 2024.

The indicative calendar for the Eurosystem’s regular tender operations incorporates the alignment of the announcement, allotment and settlement dates of three-month longer-term refinancing operations (LTROs) with the corresponding dates of main refinancing operations (MROs). The alignment of the settlement dates is aimed at facilitating rollovers from MROs to LTROs, and vice versa. The change is effective as of the LTRO to be allotted on 30 January 2024. Consequently, the maturity dates of the LTROs to be allotted in October, November and December 2023 will coincide with the new settlement dates applicable as of 2024. The calendar includes only MROs and three-month LTROs. It does not include any supplementary or ad hoc operations which may be carried out in 2024. The calendar can be downloaded from theECB’s website.

The indicative calendar for the Eurosystem’s reserve maintenance periods takes into account both the calendar for Governing Council meetings in 2024 and the calendar for regular tender operations.

The table also includes a change in the indicative calendar for reserve maintenance periods in 2023. Specifically, the end of the eighth maintenance period of 2023 has been brought forward by one week, from 6 February 2024 to 30 January 2024. The start of the maintenance period on 20 December 2023 is unchanged. The adjustment reflects a change in the schedule for Governing Council meetings in 2024.
