Prime Minister of Norway visits the ECB | N/A | European Central Bank | Press Release

The Prime Minister of Norway, Erna Solberg, visited the European Central Bank (ECB) today, along with members of her staff. She was accompanied by Elisabeth Walaas, Norwegian Ambassador to Germany.

Ms Solberg met Mario Draghi, President of the ECB, and other members of the ECB delegation for an exchange of views on current issues, in particular the state of the European economy and its outlook.
