Working group on euro risk-free rates recommends fallback arrangements for users of €STR | N/A | European Central Bank | Press Release

Today, the private sector working group on euro risk-free rates has publisheda report on €STR fallback arrangements. The report explains that the working group has assessed two options for possible fallback arrangements for €STR products.

Based on the assessment the working group has decided to recommend that market participants consider the measures that might be taken by the ECB as part of the regular review of the €STR methodology, as well as the policies and procedures to be followed in the event of the possible cessation of the €STR, along with the fallback provisions provided by the working group in theEONIA to €STR Legal Action Plan. The working group is of the opinion that this combination will provide sufficient contingency as fallback measures for the €STR.
