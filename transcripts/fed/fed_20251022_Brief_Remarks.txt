Through flexible underwriting, tailored lending, and deep community development relationships, CDFIs meet credit needs through good times and bad. CDFIs help fuel the revitalization of neighborhoods, small businesses, and local economies.

When I travel around the country as a Fed Governor, I often meet with local community leaders, including local CDFIs. I've seen countless examples of CDFIs helping to build affordable housing, daycare centers, and revitalize neighborhoods, in communities from Hartford, Connecticut to Dallas, Texas.

CDFIs operate all over the nation, including in Indian Country.
